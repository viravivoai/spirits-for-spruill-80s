About the Event

Spruill Center for the Arts
5339 Chamblee Dunwoody Rd, Dunwoody, GA 30338

This year's 40th Annual Artistic Affair builds on the electric energy of last year's 70s celebration, fast-forwarding a decade into full-on 1980s fun! Get ready to bust a move, scramble the Cube, and relive your Atari days.

Bid on totally tubular items in our live and silent auctions featuring artwork from students, instructors, and local artists! Revel in the righteous 80s hits spun by our rad DJ and join in our solo and group costume contests, raffles, and much more as we raise funds for arts education in our community.

Totally Rad Activities

Breakdancing & Electric Slide

Light up the night with neon everything! Glowsticks, blacklights, and all the fluorescent fun you can handle.

The 80s Vibe

Keith Haring was one of the decade's most recognizable artists, known for his bold, dynamic figures and simple lines that turned pop and street art into high art.

The Activation

A large roll of butcher paper or blank canvas on a wall. We'll start with Haring's signature "Radiant Baby" or "Barking Dog" figures to set the tone.

The Challenge

Thick, black Sharpie markers and neon/bright-colored paint pens. Draw your own simple, active figures and fill the gaps!

Fundraising Tie-in

At the end of the night, the mural can be cut into smaller squares to be sold as unique, collaborative art pieces!
